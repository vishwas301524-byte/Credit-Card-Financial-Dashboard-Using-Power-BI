# 💳 Credit Card Weekly Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive **Credit Card Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to monitor weekly business performance, customer behavior, revenue trends, and transaction insights. The dashboard enables financial stakeholders to make **data-driven decisions** through dynamic visualizations and real-time KPIs.

# 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project was to develop an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that provides comprehensive insights into **credit card transactions, customer demographics, and weekly business performance**.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- 📈 Monitor revenue and transaction trends
- 👥 Understand customer demographics and spending behavior
- 💳 Analyze credit card performance
- 📊 Track weekly KPIs and financial metrics
- 📍 Identify top-performing customer segments and regions
- 🎯 Support strategic business decision-making

  - Education

- 💳 Credit Card Details
  - Blue
  - Silver
  - Gold
  - Platinum

- 💰 Transaction Information
  - Transaction Amount
  - Transaction Count
  - Interest Earned

- 📅 Weekly Transaction Records

- 📍 Geographic Information

The raw data was imported from **CSV files**, loaded into **SQL**, and connected to **Power BI** for reporting and visualization.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

> 🚀 **Revenue increased by 28.8% week-over-week.**

> 💰 **Overall revenue exceeded 57 Million.**

> 👨 **Male customers generated higher revenue (31M) than female customers (26M).**

> 💳 **Blue and Silver cards contributed approximately 93% of all transactions.**

> 📍 **Texas, New York, and California generated nearly 68% of total revenue.**

> 📊 **Customer activation rate reached 57.5%.**

> ⚠️ **Delinquency rate remained low at 6.06%.**
